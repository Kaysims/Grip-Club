Classes

Our classes do not cost an extra than a regular bouldering session, no need to book just come down and join in.

Bouldring Basics

Join us every Monday at 7:00pm for our Bouldering Basics class. This class is perfect for anyone new to bouldering. It will be run by one of our instructors, and will break down all you need to know about bouldering, before starting on one of our beginner routes.

Coach and Climb

Every Thursday at 7-10pm is our coach and climb night. It is run by one of the Grip Club intructors, who will be on hand to offer coaching, tips and tricks at no extra cost.

Strength Saturdays

At Grip Club every Saturday morning between 8:00-9:00am is our strength training class. This class is aimed at targeting the muscle groups boulders use the most. It includes upper body, finger strength and grip training.

Kids Club

This is a twice a week group, running Tuesdays and Fridays from 5:00-7:00pm. Diffrent intructors for diffrent age groups from ages 6-9, ages 10-12 and ages 13-16. This class is a great way to get your kids some pysical exercise in a fun enviroment.

Social Sundays

Join us every Sunday morning from 9:00-11:00am. This is a social climb where we encourage you to meet new people at the club. After you finish climbing you can enjoy some great coffee in our Coffee Hut and catch up with friends new and old.

Hire Us

Want to hire the bouldering gym. Whether for b-day part or work team building event. Get in touch with us and we can make it happen. Contact us here!
