Classes

Our classes do not cost any extra than a regular bouldering session, no need to book, just come down and join in.

Bouldring Basics

Join us every Monday at 7:00 pm for our Bouldering Basics class. This class is perfect for anyone new to bouldering. It will be run by one of our instructors and will break down all you need to know about bouldering.

Coach and Climb

Every Thursday from 7:00-10:00 pm is our coach and climb night. It is run by one of the Grip Club instructors, who will be on hand to offer coaching, tips and tricks at no extra cost.

Strength Saturdays

At Grip Club, every Saturday morning between 8:00-9:00 am is our strength training class. This class is aimed at targeting the muscle groups boulderers use the most.

More classes...

Kids Club

Kids club run's Tuesdays and Fridays from 5:00-7:00 pm. Different instructors for different age groups from ages 6-9, ages 10-12 and ages 13-16. This class is a great way to get your kids some physical exercise in a fun environment.

Social Sundays

Join us every Sunday from 9:00-11:00 am. This is a social climb where we encourage you to meet new people at the club. After you finish climbing, you can enjoy some great coffee in our Coffee Hut and catch up with fellow boulderers.

Hire Us

Want to hire the bouldering gym? Whether for a b-day party or a team building event. Get in touch with us, and we can make it happen.
