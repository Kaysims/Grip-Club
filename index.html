About us

What is bouldering ?

Bouldering dates back to the 1800s, it was used as a playful form of training for larger ascents. Bouldering is similar to climbing except you do not use a harnesses or ropes. Its often done close to the ground, however some experienced free solo climbers haved used bouldering skills to ascended El Capitanin in Yosemite, and various other cliffs. Our indoor bouldering gym has over 100 routes with saftey mats all around to prevent injury.

Our Mission :

We are a local family run bouldering gym, located in Essex. We believe that bouldering is a great way to get fit, have fun and meet new people. We welcome all levels from new bees to expert climbers. We have 5* routes and a great coffee spot to relax and catch up with friends old and new. We hope to create a place for more people to participate in bouldering, and experiance the joy, benfits and social aspect of this great sport. Come and have a go !

Can i join with no experiance ?

Yes. All levels of experiance can enjoy bouldering. Bouldering is actually great for beginners, it is easy to get started with bouldering, despite the image of it being an athletic sport. We have lots of diffrent routes which varey in level of difficulty. If you are unsure, why not come down to one of our bouldering basics classes.

How to get involved ?

Just pop down anytime within our opening hours. If you want to meet other people who boulder we recommend coming along to our Social Sundays. If you feel you need some guidance before jumping straight in on your own, then please look at the classes we offer.
